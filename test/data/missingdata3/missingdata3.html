077 Loch Seaforth Rapids

Contributor Sean Ziehm-Stephen

Grade 3

Location NB 247 158 (58.0461, -6.679)

A 50m long tidal rapid with a standing wave in stunning surroundings, the Loch Seaforth rapids are a stalwart venue of the Stornoway Canoe Club and the introducer of many a Hebridean child to moving water. Particularly exciting in a strong southerly wind, any tidal range above 4 metres will work, but the bigger the better. Get there a couple of hours before low water and watch it appear. Park somewhere off the road at the western end of the village of Airidh a Bhruaich and paddle into the wave.
